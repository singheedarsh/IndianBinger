About Prerna

The woman who made it all!

Prerna’s passion for food is as vibrant as the spices she grew up with. From her mother’s kitchen she has carried an unshakable enthusiasm for learning and creating. She approaches every dish with curiosity and precision — always eager to explore new techniques, flavors, and traditions. Adaptable and open-minded, she embraces change as an essential ingredient in growth. Her positive energy fills every kitchen she steps into, turning challenges into inspiration and meals into moments of joy.
